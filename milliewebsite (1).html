About Me

Hello! I’m currently a student at Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP), pursuing a degree in New Media Communication. I’m a proud STPM graduate and the youngest of two siblings. I come from a unique multicultural background, being born into a family of diverse ethnicities from Sarawak. Growing up in such a rich cultural environment has shaped me into someone who values diversity, creativity, and open-mindedness. As I continue my journey in the world of media and communication, I’m excited to learn, grow, and explore new ways to tell stories that connect people.

My Hobbies

Music has always been a big part of my life. I enjoy playing different instruments during my free time, and it has become one of my favorite ways to express myself. I have the ability to play more than three musical instruments, and each one gives me a different kind of joy and creativity. Playing music not only helps me relax, but also challenges me to improve and explore new sounds.

My Family

I come from a loving and harmonious family based in Sarawak. My mother is a Bidayuh and works as a teacher, while my father is an Iban and serves as a police officer. I have one sister who is also a student at UniMAP. Growing up in a multiracial family has given me the ability to speak multiple languages and appreciate different cultures. Our home is always filled with warmth, understanding, and strong family values.

My Cat

Cincong is my beloved cat and holds a special place in my heart. He came into my life during a time when my mental health wasn’t at its best. Since then, he has taught me the true meaning of love and the importance of appreciating God’s creations. His simple, calming colors bring me a sense of peace and comfort. Having him by my side has been a beautiful and healing experience.
